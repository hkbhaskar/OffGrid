About Us


Many of us dream of living a country life free from the bustle of rigorous activity and monotony of urban lifestyle. We present automated and fully-equipped farms which require a minimal human interface. Situated less than 130 km away from Delhi NCR, it gives you the option to live your country life dream without straying too far from home.


We started our journey five years ago with the goal to offer an alternative to a sustainable lifestyle by taking you back to nature. We have now devised a project which allows interested individuals to purchase ready-to-use farms. These farms are in a gated community that encompasses various amenities within closed perimeters. Despite the pandemic still hovering as a threat, we aim to give you an escape from the stress of being confined within four walls, by bringing you close to nature.


With the rise in demand for secure communal living, the property will appreciate over time and attract the attention of investors who are willing to purchase the property even when the market is down. You can earn out of land appreciation, rentals, and harvesting.

our mission

Our mission is to promote the alternative option of sustainable and healthy living. We started our venture about five years ago with an objective to give the urban dweller a reprieve from their daily routine and the hustle-bustle of city life. We also wish to provide our guests with the choice of a healthy lifestyle in the heart of nature.

Our USP

The satisfaction of our valuable guests is of utmost importance to us and we have handpicked the best products to improve the quality of agriculture. Here are some points that highlight why we are unique and better:

We not only provide plots for agricultural land and building farmhouses, but we also provide fully equipped ready-to-use, self-sustainable fruits and vegetable farms.

We take the onus to plant the saplings and manage everything in this gated community so you can sit back and relax.

This is a tax-efficient investment, which means you are required to pay the least amount of taxes in comparison to urban real estate. Now, you can enjoy the best of both worlds.

We are committed to introducing innovative ideas to enhance our agricultural output without the use of harmful synthetic additives. We implement advanced technology to ensure that the final products are of the highest quality.

With a resort spread over 5 acres, you can retreat into the heart of nature and relax in the recreational centres. We provide luxury amenities and facilities for you to escape from the stress of urban life.

This is an asset that you can gift yourself or your loved ones, or you can even purchase it as an investment for the future generation.
