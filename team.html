Our Team

Meet The Team
Research Team

Our research team constantly works on improving the quality of the crops and conducts tests on plants. The researchers are stationed at the in-house research and development labs where they work on enhancing the productivity of the products.

Product Assessment

A team is allotted to assess the quality of the raw materials as well as the final products. They test the shape, taste, texture and smell of the resources. This is done to ensure that only the best of the products reach our guests.

Management Team

We have a team of highly qualified and experienced managers who control and maintain the entire property. They ensure all the services are carried out in the best of capacity for the utmost satisfaction of our guests. They also assist the other teams, if called upon in any matter.

Culinary Team

We also house a team of culinary experts and trained chefs in the estate. Each of them strives hard to prepare and present the best delicacies to the guests. They use fresh, authentic and unadulterated produce from the farm to cook up devourable dishes to satiate your palate.

What We Are Good At

It would be impossible to implement and manage such an intricate concept without our team, who is our most valuable asset. We have a team of extremely trained and experienced researchers and professional managers.
