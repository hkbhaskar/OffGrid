Our Services

We know you like to have choices and we have them all for you. The seeds we grow are high-yielding variety (HYV), for higher production and are well suited and proven for the local ecosystem.

Vegetable garden (seasonal)

During this time of economical crisis, investing in assets would aid your finances. With this project, you have the option to grow vegetables of your choice in your own farm and get them delivered to your home. We all agree that nothing is better than organic and fresh produce to boost our immunity.

Sustainable Power Source

We understand the responsibility to conserve non-renewable resources, hence we have designed the project in such a way that we can depend on renewable sources of energy for our power requirement.

Automated drip irrigation system

Agricultural land demands precise maintenance that may be exhausting, so we have taken care of it for you through “drip irrigation.” Drip irrigation is the most effective and time-conserving system of irrigation which can be controlled even from a long distance.

App-based real-time feedback

Technology has developed in leaps and bounds in the agricultural sector. We understand that it might be quite difficult to take time out of your busy schedule and travel back and forth to keep an eye on the condition of your crops.

Option to sell crops in-house

We give you the option to sell crops in-house. Yes, you heard that right- you can also get products procured from your crops by the in-house produce centre.

In-house oil production

There is no compromise when it comes to health and the best way to ensure good health is to consume organic products. You can now derive the satisfaction of having cold-pressed oil of the highest and purest quality.

In-house dairy

There is nothing as good as fresh dairy and we have the highest quality of dairy products in our stock. We have a wide range of fresh dairy products for you to choose from- butter, milk, cheese, clarified butter, curd, etc. We have it all in the house. The products are manufactured in the estate.

24 x 7 video surveillance

Concerned about the safety of your farm? We have installed a heightened security system and 24 x 7 surveillance to ensure a safe and secure environment. All you need do is install an app to keep an eye on your farm through your phones right where you are.

Membership to a recreational centre

Bored of the monotonous lockdown routine? We have news for you. This would not be a proper retreat if there were no recreational activities to enthuse our dear guests. The guests also have access to the recreational centre on the property.

Access to Produce Centre

Have you ever been curious about how raw materials are processed into consumables? One of the added benefits of the farm is access to the produce centre where you can witness the processing of raw materials into the final products. It includes the processing of honey, dairy products, medicinal herbs, etc.

In-house plant lab

You don’t need to be a botanist to enter the plant lab on the farm, nor do you need to know the science behind it. Why? Because we have a team of experienced researchers to guide you through the lab works and projects. The experts of the in-house plant lab conduct tests on various plants and deduce the best method for agrarian success.

Option to build in the property

A home would not be a home without a house to rest your weary bones. We give you the option to build your own nest on this property. There is nothing more peaceful than living close to nature and if you are ready to pack your stuff and get away from the city life, you can build a house right in the heart of nature.

The only way to make this happened is to take action!
